BMW Ladies Championship2021

84 players, 72-hole stroke-play, 2 million dollar in prize money.

BMW Ladies Championship will be played in LPGA International Busan 21 - 24 October 2021. The course comprise 72-hole stroke-play format and world class top players will compete for 300 thousand dollar prize money for 4 days.

I have no doubt that the golf fans will be fascinated by the combination of best female golfers and best premium brand BMW.

Sean Pyun, Chief Business Officer – Asia, LPGA
