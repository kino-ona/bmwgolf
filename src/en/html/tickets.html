BMW LADIES CHAMPIONSHIP VISITOR GUIDELINE
  • Individuals without masks and those with temperature above 37.5 degrees are prohibited from entry into the golf course
  • Face masks are required at all times
  • Please practice responsible social distancing and maintain a minimum distance of 1-meter
  • All visitors are required to complete an online health questionnaire and check-in via the QR code available via the official tournament mobile app
  • In accordance with COVID-19 government restrictions, contact among visitors and players/caddies are strictly prohibited
  • For the safety of all individuals, visitors are advised to adhere to the instructions of tournament staff at all times
  • Individuals who fail to comply with COVID-19 regulations will be required to leave the premises and no refund will be provided
BMW LADIES CHAMPIONSHIP FAQ

Q. Will the BMW Ladies Championship be open to the public in 2021?
A. Strictly adhering to the current government recommendations and guidelines on COVID 19, the competition will be held based on local social distancing levels for outdoor sports events. The competition will proceed with utmost importance placed on the safety of all participants, visitors and staff at the event. Latest information on the competition will be made available on our website and mobile app.

Q. What will it mean for BMW Ladies Championship to be hosted in 2021?
A. BMW Ladies Championship is the only LPGA tour event in South Korea where, despite the effects of COVID-19, the title sponsor BMW Korea remains committed to the cause of elevating the standard of golf in South Korea and investing in the domestic sports industry. Held for the first time in 2019, the BMW Ladies Championship attracted a spectatorship of more than 70,000 visitors from all over the world. After having to postpone the event in 2020 due to COVID-19, the decision to host the competition will not change if the event is restricted to behind closed doors. This year brings a special focus on positioning South Korea as a desired golf destination and advancing South Korean professional golfers to the international stage

Q. How can I watch the competition?
A. Live broadcast of the tournament will be available on JTBC Golf Channel and visitors may also catch the online live streaming via Naver Sports. The public may also refer to the official website and tournament social channels for updates on the competition.

Q. How can I purchase tickets to BMW Ladies Championship?
A. Should the event be staged with limited spectatorship, ground tickets and the BMW Premium Class tickets for the tournament will be made available through the official tournament app from September 23, 2021.

Q. Can I purchase tickets on-site?
A. BMW Ladies Championship ticket sales will ONLY be available via the official mobile app from September 23, 2021.
You may simply download the "BMW Ladies Championship" application from Google Play Store or the Apple Store.

Q. What is the refund policy should the event be cancelled or take place behind closed doors?
A. Refunds will be given on all purchased tickets should the competition be cancelled or proceed without spectators due to COVID-19. Click here for more information.

Q. What happens if I can’t scan my mobile ticket due to lost or damaged cell phone when I enter the Gallery parking lot?
A. Visitors would need to provide proof of purchase and personal information at the entrances located at the Gallery parking lot, Il-Gwang Station or World Cup Village parking area to be allowed entry into the golf course.
* All parking facilities will not be accessible should the event be staged behind closed doors.

Q. Where can I find my mobile ticket, mandatory health questionnaire and QR code?
A. This information will be made available to visitors via the official tournament mobile app.

Q. What mobile functions are available via the official tournament app?
A. Visitors may easily purchase their tournament tickets and gain entry to the golf course via the mobile ticket available on the app. Important tournament updates and information will also be made available via push notifications. In case of an emergency, visitors may also report their location and situation via real-time communication with the tournament officials through the mobile app. We hope visitors enjoy the only LPGA Tournament in South Korea with greater safety via the BMW Ladies Championship mobile app.

Q. How can I get to the venue after arriving at the Gallery parking lot?
A. Visitors may board the Gallery shuttle bus after a routine temperature check, completing the mobile health questionnaire, and checking in via the QR code. For the safety of all visitors, all shuttle buses will be disinfected after each trip.
*Shuttle buses will not operate should the event be staged behind closed doors.

Q. Will social distancing rules be implemented at the venue?
A. The BMW Ladies Championship places utmost priority on the health and safety of all visitors, athletes and staff. The competition will comply with the Government's guidelines on the restrictions of COVID-19. All individuals must maintain a minimum social distance of 1-meter and wear a face mask at all times.

Q. Will there be a Players’ signing event and other activities like in 2019?
A. This year’s tournament will proceed without any special signing or gallery events due to COVID-19 restrictions. Food and beverage will also not be available out on the golf course. Visitors should proceed to the Gallery Plaza F&B zone should they wish to purchase any food and beverage during the event.
*Gallery Plaza will not operate should the event be staged behind closed doors.
